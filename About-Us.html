This Is Who We Are…

Octavian is an international security and facility management company, with head office in the UK and branches in various countries in Europe, the United States, the Middle East, and Africa.

Octavian was formed in 2003 with a mission to offer clients tailored, quality led service, at an affordable price, to totally change the image of facilities management and to improve the standard of facility Management solutions in the United Kingdom.

The company extended its services to Ghana in 2016. Our range of services at Octavian Ghana offers complete facilities management solutions to clients in all types of industry.

We are a company that prides itself on training and quality of service, tailored to meet our clients’ requirements. We provide a broad range of solutions including janitorial, pest control, waste management, maintenance, reception/customer service, managing and safeguarding the assets of our clients.

We embrace the philosophies and values of our clients in all facets whilst at work.

Our Core
Values

At Octavian we see our core values as guiding principles that dictate our behaviour and action.

These Core values directly influence us in what is right from wrong, how we interact with our clients and stakeholders; they directly influence us to stay on the right path and aid us in fulfilling our business goals.

Our Core Values create an unwavering and unchanging set of principles for us to strive to, on our journey to becoming the stand out security provider in the industry. Our Core Values give us direction and therefore our Core Values must be Direct.

Dedication

To succeed in what we do everything we do goes into ensuring that we are successful for ourselves and our partners.

Innovation

Our creative and ground breaking across our business finds new and innovative solutions to give us all a competitive edge, we will always look for a creative approach and never rest on our or others laurels.

Respect

Being leaders in embracing all cultures, abilities and backgrounds encouraging everyone to have a voice and respecting the opinions, customs, traditions and beliefs of all.

Excellence

We believe in Excellence at everything we do is done at the very best of our ability and only completed when we are certain it is the best we can do.

Commitment

Safety and security is, and always will be our first priority as we continually check to ensure that what we do is safe for all and is in the best interests of ourselves and our partners.

Trust

With complete transparency we engender trust with our partners we take full accountability for our actions and operate with openness and respect throughout all areas of the business allowing our partners to trust us with their security.
